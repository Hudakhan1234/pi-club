So, now we'll talk about How to overcome Glossophobia. (GLOSSOPHOBIA= Fear of public speaking.)

I don't have any problem in sharing this with you all that I'm a glossophobic person.

  • So, in order to get rid of your glossophobia, i will provide few tips.
Although, i won't guarantee y'all that these tips will be useful everytime.
But i will recommend you to try them at least once!
  1. Stand straight and take a deep breath before speaking.
  2. Observe your audience and try to find a familiar face,
    actually more than one face(we don't want to make it look weird). and focus on them while speaking.
  3. Think about what you are going to speak, and focus on your words only.
  4. Don't be negative & just think positively.
